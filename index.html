Our Mission

Research shows the people who need donations, clothes, food and resources the most are not the people who are able to collect it from standard charities. What started as sheer dedication from our founder to bring donations straight to those in need, has inspired the rest of us into what we now call Close the Gap - where we close the gap between those who can give and those who need it the most.

We brought this up to a number of donation shops, but they all said the same thing; the overhead of this kind of operation outweighs any tangible benefit. It's a special point of view however, when we realized how much people were willing to help out - our volunteers work mostly autonomously, and our mission so far has been a great success.
